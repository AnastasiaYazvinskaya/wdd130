Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to promote healthy home eating. Here, people should be able to view various recipes and menus, and place an order for food delivery for their chosen dishes. The main page of the site should be for informational purposes - to attract visitors to a closer acquaintance with the company. Also, the site should support the ability to post new recipes so that customers have the opportunity to try new things. The site should demonstrate all the benefits of healthy nutrition as a basis for our health.

Audience

The target audience of the site is housewives who want to diversify the menu, while spending a minimum of time on it. Our clients are people who are tired of the monotony or want to improve their eating habits, and also want to avoid the fuss in order to achieve their goal. They want to make their choice quickly and go back to just cooking and be sure that the work of collecting and delivering products will be done quickly and efficiently.

Content

Home page

HomeWaiter is a company that allows everyone to become a professional chef in their home kitchen using only fresh and high-quality products. Don't know what to cook? For you, we have created the best cookbook that will be available to you at any time. Choose what you love. Choose something new and different. Feel like a real chef in your kitchen. Do not want to go to the store or think that you will buy something extra again? Order a set of products for your diet to your home. You can order products according to our recipes or assemble your own set. Forget about the shopping routine and entrust it to us. Want to know more about the food you eat? We will tell you all the secrets of products and their preparation. With our tips, you can balance your diet and improve your health. Enjoy yourself, your food and its preparation.

Images for the Home page

Recipes

Are you tired of the monotony in your diet and want to try something new? Then you are in the right place. Here we have collected our best collection of recipes for every day. Every week our chefs will provide you with new recipes that you can easily repeat yourself at home. So you can always bring something new and add more colors to your life. Choose your unique taste of the new day. And to make it easier for you to get started, you can order a set of products from us, which will be delivered directly to your home. Then just follow the detailed instructions or video and soon you will be surprised what you are capable of in the kitchen. Public recipes and the opportunity to get natural, high-quality products directly to your home are our uniqueness. Cook at home with your own hands, putting all your soul and love, and then you can get real pleasure from cooking and tasting your own culinary masterpiece.

Images for the Recipes Page

Boxes

Tired of going shopping and buying more than you need or are you prone to spending too much? Then we have found a solution for you. Here you can buy all the necessary products for your daily diet, so that you will not have anything extra. Each box contains only high-quality and natural products. For each recipe in our library, we assemble its own individual box containing all the necessary ingredients for its preparation. You can also edit each set to your liking. If you want to order a box for your recipes, then you can create your own custom box based on your personal cookbook. Check out our recipes and select the appropriate boxes or build your own box for take-out and cooking.
